Bugbot

by John VanSickle
DESCRIPTION OF HOW THIS ANIMATION WAS CREATED:

I took an old model and did the math to articulate the joints so that they followed the path of the feet. The ground uses a normal_map and the repeat warp in order to have the granite pattern appear to repeat at regular intervals. I would have used a tiled floor, but everyone's tired of that, I'm sure, and the surface I used made for a smaller .MPG file.
